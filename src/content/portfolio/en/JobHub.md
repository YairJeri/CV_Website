JobHub is an innovative online platform designed to transform job searching and simplify the hiring process. Users can create profiles, log in, and discover job opportunities that match their skills and experience. With an intuitive and modern interface, applicants can easily find and apply to positions that fit their interests, while companies can efficiently manage candidate selection processes.

The purpose of JobHub is to offer a comprehensive solution that optimizes the way job opportunities are searched and managed. The platform aims to enhance the user experience for both job seekers and employers through a smooth and secure interface, facilitating access to jobs and contributing to an effective reduction of unemployment.

**Role:** Full Stack Developer  
**Contribution:** Developed the backend API using Java and Spring, and implemented the Angular frontend for job seekers and employers. Integrated authentication, job filtering, and user profile management and websockets for communication between users.
